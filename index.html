The Iron Yard Tahiti Relocates to New Beachfront Office Location

by Julian Wilson, The Iron Yard Papeete Campus Director

Today The Iron Yard Papeete was pleased to announce that we have moved into a new modern beachfront office location on Palm Tree Road just next to Surfer's Cove Beach.

The first class to begin there will be a Front-End Engineering cohort relocated from Cincinnati, Ohio.
